About T-eco-evo

This research group started off in April 2016 and is embedded in the Department of Theoretical Biology at the Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Biology.

The focus of this group is to explore the ecological as well as evolutionary processes underpinning biological phenomena.

Focal topics include, eco-evolutionary game dynamics and its application to social evolution, antagonistic interactions patters (host-parasite, predator-prey relationships) and symbiotic intereactions (mutualisms, between and within species).
